The abilities of a programmer ressemble those of an essayist: #

  • Orthography1 and Grammar
  • Observation
  • Research
  • Analysis
  • Pleasing expressiveness

And here’s the list of talents vital to programmers: #

  • The ability to read and understand a problem description to grasp the formulator’s desires.
  • The ability to extract difficulties and ignore irrelevancies.
  • The ability to recognize where theory can be applied and the discretion2 to apply it.
